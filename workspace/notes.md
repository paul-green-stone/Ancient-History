# Notes

## Color palette

For Egypt, the color palette will use $07 $37 $27 $17 for the colors of the floor tiles

For Greece, the colors will be $0E $3D $10 $2D for the floor tiles

For china the colors will be $0E $17 $18 $27

BG - background
SF - surface
HI - highlights
SH - shadows

WORLD   BG  SF  HI  SH
EGYPT   07  37  27  17
GREEK   0E  3D  10  2D
CHINA   01  18  28  08
